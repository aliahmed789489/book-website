Sketches

Sketches are a versatile and expressive form of art, allowing artists to capture their ideas and visions in a rapidly executed freehand drawing. Whether created with traditional mediums like graphite, charcoal, or pastel, or through digital tools and apps like Tayasui Sketches, sketches serve various purposes. 04

Line Art

Line art is a style of drawing that consists of distinct straight lines or curves placed against a plain background, without gradations in shade or color. It can be created using different techniques, such as technical illustrations with constant or varying line widths, brushwork, engraving, or even as caricatures, cartoons, ideographs, or glyphs . Dubu Publishers have a team of line art illustrators who can transform your book to a masterpiece.
